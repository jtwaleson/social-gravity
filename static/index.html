Social Gravity

Social Gravity is a new way to analyze social networks on twitter. I'm sure it could be useful for marketeers and egomaniacs, but more importantly: it's magical.

If you've read this far, you should follow me on twitter: @jtwaleson. After all, I made all this, and it works with twitter

How do I ....

Click the @ button. Enter a twitter account that is not protected, like @jtwaleson. Leave the settings to default, press Go!

Your friends will now begin to trickle into the screen. Want to see something cool? Press the start/stop button. Your friends that know eachother will flock together.

Want to see something even more cool? Use your scroll wheel on the page.

More? Ok, drag one of your around and see who follows him/her!

Still more? What about this: press the '?' button. You will see keywords that are being represented in one area.

No way, there is even more: click a couple of people to see who they are connected to.
